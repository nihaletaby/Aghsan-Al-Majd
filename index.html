Trust & Quality

Today We Start With Quality ... We Become The Best Tomorrow With Confidence

Aghsan Al-Majd Food Industries Factory is a factory specialized in vegetable oils was established in the middle of 1439 AH, has achieved high sales as the monthly production of the factory reaches 60000 tanks and we cover all regions of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.
We are careful to provide the latest and best technologies at the factory to obtain the best quality, and the business is supervised by a group of specialized administrators and supervisors. We at Al-Majd Branch factory do our best to build confidence with our customers in the quality of our products to become success partners and an integral part of our family.

Vision
